Learn more about Corneal Blindness in your country

Over 10 million people have corneal blindness. Imagine every person in Los Angeles not being able to see.

Over 98% of people living with corneal blindness live in developing countries. A standard operation can transform their lives.

Working with partners in more than 30 countries, SightLife is increasing access to care and the number of corneal transplants performed each year to transform millions of lives.
