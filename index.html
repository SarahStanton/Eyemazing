Working to eliminate Corneal
Blindness worldwide by 2040

Over 10 million people have corneal blindness. Imagine every person in Los Angeles not being able to see.

Over 98% of people living with corneal blindness live in developing countries.

In India, there are 1.1 million people who can’t see out of either eye and another 5 million who can’t see out of one eye because of corneal blindness.
